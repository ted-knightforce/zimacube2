ZFS ARC — Warm Cache Effect: With a warm ARC, glacier random 4K read IOPS climb from 14,781 → 83,588 — a 5.6× uplift at 1.48 ms avg latency. Session hit rate: 99.9%. The 14× cold IOPS gap narrows to ~2.5× for workloads with repeated read patterns. Day 12 cumulative ARC hit rate: 96.9%. ARC does not affect write performance — write benchmarks reflect hardware throughput regardless of cache state.
