ZFS ARC — Warm Cache Effect: With a warm ARC, glacier random 4K read IOPS climb from 14,781 → 83,929 — a 5.7× uplift at 1.48 ms avg latency. Session hit rate: 100.0%. The 14× cold IOPS gap narrows to ~2.5× for workloads with repeated read patterns. Cumulative ARC hit rate: 93.7%. ARC does not affect write operations — write charts show direct hardware throughput only.
